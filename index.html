Conservation AI · Field Research

Mount Kenya Elephant Corridor — Wildlife Detection Model

Trained a custom YOLOv8 object-detection model on ~5,000 annotated camera-trap images across three sites in the 14 km Lewa–Ngare Ndare corridor. Built Python post-processing scripts for batch inference, centroid-based movement classification, and CSV export. Achieved ~91% accuracy for elephants.
